## Executive Summary
Acme Retail Co is positioned for growth with targeted improvements in inventory accuracy, omnichannel fulfillment efficiency, and customer analytics.

## Business Profile
- Name: Acme Retail Co
- Industry: Retail
- Employees: 28
- Annual Revenue: $1,750,000.00

## Current State Analysis
Acme operates two brick-and-mortar stores with a growing e-commerce channel. Systems include a POS, spreadsheet-based inventory counts, and a basic email marketing platform.

## Key Findings
- Stock accuracy below 90% leads to lost sales and over-ordering
- Order picking is manual with limited batching and routing
- Customer data is siloed between in-store and online systems

## Recommendations
- Implement cycle counting with ABC classification and barcode scanning
- Introduce batch picking and standardized packing workflows
- Consolidate customer data and build RFM segmentation

## Next Steps
- Run a 4-week pilot for cycle counting in the top 20% SKUs
- Document current fulfillment steps and measure baseline KPIs
- Evaluate a lightweight CDP or data warehouse for unification

## Challenges to Address
- Inconsistent inventory accuracy
- Rising fulfillment costs
- Limited customer insights
